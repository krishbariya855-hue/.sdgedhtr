Premium Red Onion Specifications

MAHIVERSE GLOBLE’s Dehydrated Premium Red Onion Powder is precisely engineered to solve the texturing and shelf instability issues that disrupt high-velocity culinary lines. By bypassing heavy thermal roasting steps, our advanced multi-stage moisture extraction framework protects the natural fibers, keeping the underlying pungent flavor and sweet notes vibrant.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Red Onion Powder?

Red Onion Powder is derived from clean, sorted red onions that undergo controlled low-temperature moisture removal before being pulverized into fine, kitchen-ready granulated spices.

How should I store the product?

Keep the product sealed inside its original moisture-proof pouch. Store in a cool, dark place away from ambient steam or direct environmental humidity to block clumping.

Do you supply in bulk container volumes?

Yes. Equipped with all legal frameworks (including valid GST and active Import Export Codes), MAHIVERSE GLOBLE manages full maritime cargo container shipments seamlessly across international borders.

Are there anti-caking elements added to the powder?

No. We process our agricultural powders with zero artificial fillers, flow enhancements, chemical additions, or starches, locking in 100% product purity.
