Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Because agricultural commodities, dehydrated spice powders, and organic fertilizers are perishable food and farm items, returns are managed under specific quality checking guidelines.

1. Damaged or Non-Conforming Deliveries

If your package arrives physically compromised or exhibits moisture seal defects, report the problem to our team within 48 hours of destination receipt along with clear photo or video proof.

2. Corporate Bulk Freight Dispute Resolution

Commercial bulk container logistics are strictly governed by pre-shipment laboratory parameters. Quality claims must be backed by official testing agency reports within 7 days of customs discharge.

3. Processing Approved Refunds

Once a product defect claim is verified, our billing team will process your return payment within 5 to 7 business days directly back to your original transaction payment account or commercial bank line.
