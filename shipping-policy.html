Shipping & Export Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

MAHIVERSE GLOBLE operates standardized domestic retail tracking and certified commercial global export logistics protocols to deliver our products efficiently.

1. Dispatch Timelines

Standard packaging orders are dispatched from our central production nodes within 2 to 3 business days. Commercial wholesale or specialized international cargo freight timelines are explicitly structured based on standard Letter of Credit (LC) and invoice schedules.

2. Global Freight & Port Documentation

We ship internationally to global regions (including the UAE and UK). All maritime or air cargo comes verified with a clear Bill of Lading, clean Certificate of Origin, phytosanitary checks, and proper customs processing sheets.

3. Duties and Import Levies

For consumer retail orders, the buyer remains responsible for any regional destination cross-border custom duties or international import taxes applied at arrival ports.

4. Loss Mitigation & Transit Tracking

Once dispatched, tracking links are systematically generated. All corporate cargo is protected under marine freight insurance structures to maintain safe global logistics routes.
